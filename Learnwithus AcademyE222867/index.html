ABOUT US

ACADEMY BEYAND 2024

Gathering momentum from the promising journey over the past successful years, LearnWithUs Academy sets the learning experience to the next level with Phase II. This giant step in crafting a future of greater impact gives unwavering faith to envision the academy’s vision and purpose from a broader perspective.

The Phase 2 developments in another 15 acres of land adjoining the existing premises are currently in progress and believed to be a key driver of LearnWithUs Academy’s future growth to further expand its services and opportunities to the generations to come as well as to impact the world at large.

  • New Faculty Building Complex
  • Hostel facilities for over 1,000 students
  • Multipurpose Conference Hall with over 1,500 seating capacity
  • Large Playground
  • Scenic Walking Paths
  • Rainwater Harvesting Pond
  • Extended Car Parking facilities
